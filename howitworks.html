How It Works

1. Register an Account

To begin using R9Coin, you need to register an account. During registration, you will be given a Holder ID. This Holder ID will be used for logging into your account.

2. Login with Holder ID

Once registered, you can login using your Holder ID and leave the Coin ID field blank for the first time. This will log you into your account.

3. Buy R9Coin

To buy R9Coin, you will need to place an order through the platform by entering the quantity of R9Coins you wish to purchase. This will send a request to the R9Coin system.

After submitting the request, we will arrange to meet in person, where you will pay the sum of your requested R9Coins.

4. Receive R9Coin

Once the payment is made in person, the corresponding amount of R9Coins will be credited to your account. At this point, I will also provide you with a Coin ID, which will serve as your new password for future logins.

5. Selling R9Coin

If you want to sell R9Coin, you can do so through the platform by placing a sell request. After submitting the request, we will meet in person, and I will give you the money for the R9Coins you've sold.

6. Secure Transactions

Remember, all transactions must be completed in person. This ensures the security of both the buyer and the seller.
